Harambe

1999 - 2016

Hi, as you may know I am Harambe. I was born May 27th, 1999 and tragically killed on May 28th, 2016, one day after my 17th birthday. I lived a peaceful and honest life in the Cincinatti Zoo. A little human walked in on my enclosure and, while trying to defend myself from the human, I was shot. I may be dead, but I live on in the memory of billions.
